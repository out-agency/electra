Still got a question?
To sign up for Electra, you will need to download the Electra app from the App Store or Google Play Store. Once you have the app, you can create an account by providing some basic personal information and payment details.
To find an Electra e-vehicle, open the Electra app and use the in-app map to locate the nearest available vehicle. When you find the Electra you want to ride, scan the QR code on the handlebars to unlock it.
Yes, you must have a valid driver's license to use Electra's e-moped sharing service.
Electra operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You can use the app to locate and reserve an e-moped at any time.
Payments for Electra rides are made through the app using a credit or debit card. You will be charged for the time you have the Electra, as well as any additional fees such as late return or parking violations.
If you experience any issues during your Electra ride, you can contact Electra's customer support team through the app or website. The team will be able to assist you with any problems you may be experiencing.
